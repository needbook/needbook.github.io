About

Needbook is an application designed for mobile-devices readers. It will be available on iOS & Android while also having a Web Application.

Library

Keep track of the books you are reading and those you have already read.

Information

Access detailed informations about characters, places & events that happend in the book.

Reader

Access additional informations of the book while reading with a simple click.

Community

Share your reading experience with our community.
